London-based charity helping those affected by natural disaster and poverty in Bangladesh.

Who we are

We are a London, UK based charity. We get in touch directly with Bangladeshi organisations or people that provide for victims of disaster by flood, cyclone and people effected by war, providing them with funds, so they can purchase dry food, clothing, temporary accommodation or any basic amenities.

We are registered with the Charities commission of England and Welsh.

What we do

  • We make grants to individuals.
  • We Make grants to organisations.

If an organisation or an individual promotes helping the poor, the sick, homeless or those in financial need as a result of war or natural disaster and can prove this, we will aid them.

Goals

The Charity's objects are for the benefit of the disadvantaged, victims of disaster by flood, cyclone and people effected by war anywhere in the world particularly people of Bangladesh who are in hardship by:

  1. Relieving poverty, sickness and distress as a result of floods, cyclones and natural calamities by providing monetary support and where necessary by providing physical support.
  2. Improving the quality of life of those victims of disaster by providing monetary support and where necessary by providing physical support.

We help anyone who is a victim of war and natural disasters. We help the homeless, the poor, the ill and anyone in need by:

  • Providing housing for the homeless in Bangladesh and the world.
  • Providing medical care and facilities to the sick and ill in Bangladesh and the world.
  • Providing financial help to people in need and suffering from poverty.
  • Providing financial help to organisations in Bangladesh that strictly aim to aid the poor, sick, or homeless.

Membership

Membership fee is £60 per annum. This means the fee can be £5 per month for 12 months. Which is definitely what most people can afford.

However, those that benefit from the charity's works, such as the people in poverty in Bangladesh do not need to pay the organisation anything.
